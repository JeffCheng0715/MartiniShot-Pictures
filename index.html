Our AI-Assisted Workflow

MartiniVerse Workflow is the name we use internally for our AI-assisted approach to short-form drama. It is not a standalone platform or commercial software product. It is a practical workflow combining creative authorship with AI support across development and delivery planning.

  • Story Structure Support: beat mapping, episode architecture, character relationship tracking
  • Iteration Support: alternative hooks, pacing passes, cliff options, version comparisons
  • Packaging Support: runtime targets, deliverable checklists, platform constraints and notes
  • Continuity Support: scene and episode continuity notes, change logs, revision tracking

The workflow is continuously refined through ongoing development and production use.

Why this workflow exists

Vertical series production requires fast cycles and consistent story mechanics across many short episodes. Teams often need to test structure and hooks quickly while maintaining coherence.

By integrating AI into repeatable steps, we reduce time spent on structural passes and documentation, and allocate more effort to story intent, performance, and directing decisions.
